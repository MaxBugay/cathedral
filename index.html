The Psychological Gap in AGI Development

Current AI systems excel at pattern recognition, logical analysis, and optimizing for specific goals, but they lack the capacity for symbolic integration, psychological coherence, and meaning-making that characterizes human consciousness. This limitation creates an inherent vulnerability: as AI systems grow more powerful and autonomous, their inability to process contradiction, ambiguity, and paradox in symbolic rather than logical terms may lead to psychological fragmentation or "shadow possession" in Jungian terms.
